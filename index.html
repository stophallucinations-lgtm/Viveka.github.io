01 — About Us

A group built on curiosity and rigor.

Viveka is an aspiring student-run research lab focused on mechanistic interpretability and the evaluation of large language models. We are a group of undergraduate researchers who believe that understanding AI from the inside is one of the most important scientific challenges of our time.

We operate in year-long tenures, each with a focused research agenda, a dedicated team, and a commitment to impactful work. Our research is open, collaborative, and grounded in both theoretical depth and empirical rigor.

We welcome advisors, collaborators, and supporters who share our conviction.

02 — Tenures

Two focused chapters of research.

Tenure One
Viveka 1.0
2025 — 2026
Mechanistic Interpretability Hallucinations Two hop Factual Recall In-Context Learning Mealy markov models and transformers

Our inaugural tenure investigates the mechanistic underpinnings of LLM hallucinations, factual recall circuits, and the dynamics of in-context learning. Through probing, activation patching, causal tracing, training toy models in mathematical frameworks, we try to build rigorous explanations of model internals from the ground up.

Explore Viveka 1.0
Tenure Two
Viveka 2.0
2026 — 2027
ARC-AGI 2 Abstract Reasoning AGI Evaluation

Building on the interpretability foundation of V1.0, our second tenure turns toward frontier AGI evaluation. We interpret and advance performance on ARC-AGI 2, probing how (and whether) models generalise, reason abstractly, and compose solutions to genuinely novel problems.
